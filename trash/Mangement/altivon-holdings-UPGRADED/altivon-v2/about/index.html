Who We Are

Technology That Doesn't Leave Local Business Behind

Altivon Holdings was founded with a singular belief: that businesses in smaller cities like Chandauli, Mirzapur, Varanasi, and Sonbhadra deserve the same quality of technology as businesses in Mumbai or Delhi.

We started small — a few passionate developers who understood the local market, spoke the local language, and believed in the power of technology to transform lives. Today, we've grown into a full-service technology company serving clients across India and around the world.

Our mission has always been the same: deliver honest, quality work on time, and stand by it. No fluff, no excuses — just great software and genuine relationships.

Whether you're a kirana store in Chandauli taking your first step online, or a startup in the UK needing a custom web app — we bring the same dedication, skill, and care to every project.
