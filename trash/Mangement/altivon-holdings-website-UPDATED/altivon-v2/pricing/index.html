Pricing FAQ

Common Questions

How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major payment methods. For projects above ₹20,000, we follow a 50% advance + 50% on delivery structure.
Is the starting price the final price?
Starting prices are for standard projects. Before we begin, we provide a detailed written quote based on your specific requirements — no surprises after.
What does "3 months free support" mean?
After your project launches, we fix any bugs or issues at no extra charge for 3 months. After that, you can subscribe to a maintenance plan or pay per request.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your monthly plan at any time. Pro-rated billing means you only pay for what you use.
Do you offer discounts for NGOs or schools?
Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, NGOs, and first-time business owners. Contact us to discuss your situation.
What if I'm not happy with the final result?
We include revision rounds in every project. If you're genuinely unhappy after all revisions, we offer a partial refund — though this has never happened in our history.
