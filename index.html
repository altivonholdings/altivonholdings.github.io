Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our services and process

What services does Altivon Holdings provide?

Altivon Holdings is a full-service website development company in India. We specialize in e-commerce websites, corporate sites, real estate portals, educational platforms, and digital strategy. We also offer custom web application development and SEO services.

Is Altivon Holdings based in India?

Yes, Altivon Holdings is an India-based website development company with a strong presence in Uttar Pradesh. We serve clients across India and globally, offering remote collaboration and on-time delivery.

Do you offer custom website development?

Absolutely. We do not use templates; every website we build is custom-coded to match your business requirements, brand identity, and scalability needs. We use modern tech stacks including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, and various CMS options.

How long does it take to develop a website?

Typical turnaround time is 12 days for a standard business website. E-commerce and complex platforms may take 3-4 weeks. We follow an agile process with regular updates.
