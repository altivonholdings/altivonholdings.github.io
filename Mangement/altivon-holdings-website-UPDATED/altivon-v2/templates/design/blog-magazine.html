India's Tech Sector
Reaches Record High
in 2025

By Rahul Sharma·March 11, 2025·8 min read

The Indian technology sector has surpassed all previous records this quarter, driven by a surge in AI adoption, fintech expansion, and a new wave of startup unicorns emerging from tier-2 cities like Varanasi, Indore, and Coimbatore. Analysts attribute this unprecedented growth to a combination of government policy support and the rising digital literacy among India's rural population.
