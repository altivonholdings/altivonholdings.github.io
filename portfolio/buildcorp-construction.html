Project Overview

Client: BuildCorp Construction, a sister company of BuildCorp, specializing in large‚Äëscale infrastructure and commercial projects.

Challenge: They needed a dedicated website to showcase their construction projects, list equipment, and allow potential clients to request quotes. The site had to convey trust, safety standards, and technical expertise.

Solution: We built a custom WordPress theme with an orange and grey colour scheme to reflect their brand. The site features a project gallery with timeline integration, an equipment inventory, and a quote request form that emails the sales team.

Project Showcase with Timeline

Each project has its own page with high‚Äëres images, project timeline (start/end dates), budget, and key milestones. Visitors can filter projects by type (residential, commercial, industrial) or location.

Equipment Inventory

A searchable database of all machinery and equipment available for projects. Includes specs, photos, and availability status. This builds trust and helps clients understand our capabilities.

Intelligent Quote Form

The quote form asks for project details, timeline, and budget. Submissions go to the sales team and are also logged in the CRM. We added conditional fields to make the form smarter (e.g., show relevant questions based on project type).
